What's inside

Built for the people who actually use it

No forms to fill. No jargon. Just the prescription in front of you, and the AI doing the typing.

📷

Camera-first capture

Take a photo. Claude Vision extracts patient name, age, mobile, doctor and tests. Staff just confirm with a tap.

🎙️

Voice fill, bilingual

Tap the mic on any field. Dictate in Hindi or English. No typing a rural patient's name letter-by-letter.

🧬

Doctor-aware test suggestions

Referred by a gynecologist? App suggests the common panel. Cardiologist? Cardiac markers. Less clicking, fewer mistakes.

🧾

Flat, clear billing

MRP plus manual discount. Split payments across cash, UPI and card. No hidden rate plans.

📱

QR on every bill

Patient walks home with a QR. Comes back to check status — staff scan it, see every test and deliver the report.

💬

WhatsApp reports

When the pathologist signs off, the PDF goes straight to the patient's WhatsApp. Automatic. Audited. Re-sendable.

📊

Owner dashboard

Revenue, payment mode breakdown, referring doctor volume, lab TAT — every rupee and every test, live.

🔬

Role-based simplicity

Billing staff see only billing. Lab sees the worklist. Pathologist signs off. Owner sees everything. No cross-role noise.

🏷️

Barcode labels

Scannable Code 128 labels auto-print for every sample tube. No more hand-written tags.

How it works

From prescription to report

A typical visit. Under two minutes.

1

Snap the prescription

Open the app, hit camera, point at the slip. Take photo.

2

AI fills the form

Name, age, gender, mobile, doctor, tests — all extracted. Confidence shown per field.

3

Staff verify & pay

Tap to correct red fields. Select payment mode. Collect. QR and receipt print.

4

Report on WhatsApp

Pathologist signs off. Report auto-dispatches. Patient scans QR any time to re-download.
